How to Make a PB and J Sandwhich

Ingredients

  • Peanutbutter
  • Jelly
  • Your preferance bread

Directions

  1. Get your ingredients together and a plate and a butter knife
  2. Take your two slices of bread and place them on your plate
  3. Then dip your knife into the peanutbutter jar and spread evenly on the bread slices
  4. After put jelly on top of the peanutbutter spread and evenly distribute the jelly
  5. Finally smoosh the bread slices together and cut in half
  6. Enjoy!

Prep Time 3 minutes.
